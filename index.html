What is ERR_404 4.0?

ERR_404 4.0 is the fourth iteration of 24hrs State Level Hackathon organised by Programmers Club of Computer Department of M. H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering

We call out all the cybernerds to come forth, compile your minds and execute your ideas in a competitive environment.
The hackathon will run across the weekend which offers students the chance to work in groups, brainstorm ideas, and build a working prototype before the time limit!
Whether it's your first hackathon, or your 50th, we would love to see students of all skill levels and from all colleges come along for a weekend of creativity, collaboration and coding.
Godspeed!

Rules and regulations!

Teams can be anywhere from two to four members. To ensure a level field for all contestants, all code, design and assets must be created during the duration of the Hackathon. You are, however, free to make plans, create wireframes, and brainstorm prior to the event. You are allowed to use 3rd party services, APIs, open source projects, libraries, and frameworks.
There will be no alcohol, smoking allowed at the event. Behave professionally.
Remember that harassment and racist, sexist, or exclusionary jokes are not appropriate for this event*. Attendees violating these rules may be asked to leave the Hackathon at the sole discretion of the Hackathon organizers. Thank you for helping make this a welcoming, friendly event for all.

General Guidelines

We are hopeful that you all will be very excited about the ERR_404 4.0 Hackathon. To conduct this event smoothly & in a successful manner. We would like you to follow the below guidelines:
  • Please be present on time for the online onsite registration.
  • Keep valid college ID card by your side for verification. Without it, admission won't be allowed.
  • Participants are expected to follow the formal/proper dress code
  • The schedule is tentative. It may change a bit,so participants are expected to abide as per the changed schedule.
  • Upload your code on github and update it from time to time.
  • Check your Internet connection, Camera and Audio prior to the start of Hackathon.
  • Keep your Camera ON at every stage of Evaluation and presentation.

    • The problem statements will be released two days before the event.

    FAQ's


    • Who can participate?
      Anyone with a knack of programming and solving problems can participate.
    • How much does it cost?
      Thanks to our college, attendance is completely free. We will also provide you with WiFi, breakfast, lunch, snacks, dinner and a workspace.
    • What should I bring?
      Bring your laptop, charger and other gadgets suitable to your project and a will to code. Please bring a valid ID for admission.
    • Where is it?
      M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering, Byculla is happy to provide space in their campus to organize the Hackathon. You can find the directions in the Venue section.
    • Will Jain Food be provided?
      Yes, food arrangements will be made for Jain Participants.
    • Is it compulsory that all members belong to same college?
      No it is not, you can form a team of students from different colleges. While registering you can either put the Team Leader's College or mutually decide.
